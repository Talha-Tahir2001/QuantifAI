About QuantifAI

QuantifAI is a modern object detection app built with Angular 19, powered by Google Gemini AI, and styled with daisyUI for a delightful user experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Your images are processed securely and are not stored after detection.

Which objects can be detected?

QuantifAI can detect a wide range of common objects, depending on the Gemini model's capabilities.

Can I use QuantifAI on mobile devices?

Yes, the app is fully responsive and works great on all devices.

Is QuantifAI free to use?

Yes, QuantifAI is open source and free for everyone.

How accurate is the object detection?

Detection accuracy depends on the quality of the image and the Gemini AI model, but it is state-of-the-art for most common objects.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions. Visit our GitHub repository to get started.

Does QuantifAI support batch image processing?

Currently, QuantifAI processes one image at a time. Batch processing is on our roadmap.

What browsers are supported?

QuantifAI works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
